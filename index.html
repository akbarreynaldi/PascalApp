About Us

Founded in 2017, ENIGMA CAMP was originally a subsidiary of PT. Square Techno Indonesia (STI) until 2019 developed into a new company, PT. Enigma Cipta Humanika.

Enigma Camp is engaged in IT Bootcamp and IT talent management. We have been in the information technology field for the last four years and our client list includes well-known companies. We work based on experience in developing software engineers who can support your business with 3 main programs, namely: Talent as a Service, Training as a Service and Experienced Hiring.
